Services

Partner Sourcing & Deal Brokerage

We identify and structure high-fit partnerships where collaboration can materially change outcomes. Our work goes beyond introductions, focusing on early qualification, alignment, and clear next steps.

Typical engagements include:

  • Strategic, commercial, and technical partner identification
  • Partner qualification and incentive alignment
  • Structured introductions with defined intent and ownership
  • Support through early alignment and pre-transaction phases

We work selectively and engage only where partnerships are actionable.

Funding & Capital Advisory

We support funding strategies where capital directly enables execution. Our role is to structure credible funding paths and engage suitable funding partners when readiness is established.

Typical engagements include:

  • Private and hybrid investor sourcing
  • Strategic funding partner identification
  • Public funding readiness, including EU programmes
  • Consortium structuring and partner role definition

We engage where funding logic, timing, and structure are realistic.

Strategic & Deal Preparation

Before outreach, we help clarify deal logic, constraints, and risks to ensure engagement is focused and credible.

Typical engagements include:

  • Partner and ecosystem strategy
  • Deal structure and collaboration design
  • Positioning, narrative, and outreach materials
  • Risk, feasibility, and stakeholder alignment

This work often precedes partner sourcing or funding engagement.

Approach

We work through a structured, gated process designed to reduce wasted effort and protect credibility on all sides.

Assessment

We clarify objectives, constraints, decision authority, and success criteria. If an opportunity is not brokerable yet, we say so early.

Deal & Path Design

Targeted Engagement

We identify and engage a limited number of high-fit partners, investors, or programmes, validating alignment early.

Alignment & Progression

Where interest is real, we support early alignment and the transition toward concrete commitments.

Engagements can run as focused sprints or ongoing support, depending on complexity and timelines.

About

We are an independent advisory and brokerage practice focused on partner sourcing and funding in situations where structure, credibility, and execution matter.

We work primarily with SMEs, scale-ups, and emerging consortiums, and selectively support more complex institutional collaborations.

Our approach combines a practical deal mindset with the rigour required in regulated, industrial, and programme-driven environments. We are selective by design and prioritise fit, clarity, and execution over volume.

We operate with discretion and respect for confidentiality.
